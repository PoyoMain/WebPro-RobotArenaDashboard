Jackpot

Jackpot is a four-wheel drive, box-shaped robot armed with a large vertical spinner made up of a drum sandwiched between two diamond-shaped disks. This configuration, dubbed the 'Die-monds', could be swapped out for a pair of heart-shaped disks called the 'Heartbreaker'. It also featured six ½" thick forks at the front, which were hinged and designed to slide underneath opponents.
